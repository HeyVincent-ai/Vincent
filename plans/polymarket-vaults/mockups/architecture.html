Invision — System Architecture

How Twitter personalities drive Polymarket bets through AI-powered vaults. Signals are ingested in real time, enriched by Claude, matched to prediction markets, and executed autonomously through per-personality vault structures.

Signal Sources

Twitter/X personalities whose posts are monitored in real time. Each personality maps to a dedicated vault with its own risk profile and market domain.

Ingestion Pipeline

Tweets are streamed, analyzed by Claude for intent and claims, matched to live Polymarket questions, risk-scored, then executed as trades.

Execution Venue

Polymarket's Central Limit Order Book (CLOB) and AMM provide the execution layer. Markets resolve on-chain with deterministic outcomes.

Vault Structure

Each personality has an isolated vault holding USDC. Users deposit into specific vaults and receive proportional P&L distributions on resolution.

Vincent Infrastructure

Handles auth, key management, wallet abstraction, and on-chain settlement. Shown as dashed lines — it wraps the entire system as the orchestration layer.

Built on Vincent — the AI agent infrastructure layer for autonomous on-chain execution.
